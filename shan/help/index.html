This Unicode character picker allows you to produce or analyse runs of Shan text. Character pickers are especially useful for people who don't know a script well, as characters are displayed in ways that aid identification.

If something is broken or missing raise an issue. For version information see the Github commit list.

About the chart

Includes characters in the Unicode v10 Myanmar block used for writing Shan.

Controls above the text area

Transliterate. Converts the contents of the text area to a Latin transliteration that provides a one-to-one correspondence between Shan and Latin symbols.

The transliteration scheme was developed for this picker, and, where possible, uses symbols that represent sounds typically associated with their Shan equivalents, rather than the sometimes fairly arbitrary selection of diacritics found in other systems. Of course, the complexities of mapping graphemes to sounds means that this is far from a real phonetic transcription, and it doesn't show inherent vowels, but the process is reversible (unlike most transcriptions).
